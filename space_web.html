ABOUT US

We don’t build spacecraft or plan extraordinary research space missions. What we do is work with the experts in order to bring you the opportunity to book the most amazing space travel packages tailored exactly how you want it.

Our expertise lies in the luxury tourism sector; our sister company has been in the private jet business for 15 years. Therefore our experience in providing the most exclusive private jet travel has enabled us to build a team of dedicated experts who understand the space travel arena like no one else, and whose attention to detail is second to none.

Our mission is to give you the most extraordinary space travel experience that will stay with you for a lifetime.

Our knowledge and experience enables us to identify and create the most sought after spaceflight adventures just for you.

We can facilitate private missions or enable you to fly on spacecraft already scheduled to launch, the possibilities are endless. No matter which spaceflight adventure you choose, you can be sure that we will create a uniquely bespoke experience which will greatly surpass your expectations.

Astronauts commonly talk about the “Overview Effect”. This is seeing the Earth as a whole, recognising it as one planet, with sustained life. The Overview Effect provides those fortunate enough to travel to space a change in perspective.

“It’s hard to explain how amazing and magical this experience is. First of all, there’s the astounding beauty and diversity of the planet itself, scrolling across your view at what appears to be a smooth, stately pace… no amount of prior study or training can fully prepare anybody for the awe and wonder this inspires.”

Kathryn D. Sullivan (Astronaut)

Space travel is becoming increasingly realistic. There are space training centres for your zero gravity experience, a space station hotel, private space shuttles and plenty of activities for you to experience in such a unique and unexplored environment.

Space is for everybody. It’s not just for a few people in science or math, or for a select group of astronauts. That’s our new frontier out there, and it’s everybody’s business to know about space.”

Christa McAuliffe (Astronaut)
